Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Orbit Crypto ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Orbit Crypto.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application named Orbit Crypto (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

Orbit Crypto is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account to use the application's core features. We do not store your name, email address, or phone number on our servers.

Portfolio Data

All data related to your cryptocurrency holdings, transactions, and portfolio balance is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this sensitive financial data to any external server.

Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (Fingerprint or Face ID), this process handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not collect, store, or transmit your biometric data.

Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain information automatically when you use our App, such as:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the hardware model, operating system and version.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our App to improve performance and user experience.

To provide specific functionalities, we integrate with third-party services. These third parties may access data necessary to perform their functions but are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

  • Firebase: Used for analytics and crash reporting to help us improve the stability of the App. Learn more about Firebase Privacy and Security.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App.

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
